About Me

I am the Co-founder and CTO of OpenJobs AI, where I lead technological innovation in AI-driven recruitment solutions. I received my Ph.D. from Purdue University, focusing on building secure, robust, and efficient large-scale AI systems.

My research interests lie at the intersection of system design, network optimization, and security. I am passionate about solving complex problems in the following areas:

  • Decentralized & Federated Learning: Resource allocation and straggler mitigation.
  • Robust Machine Learning: Defending against vulnerabilities in federated systems.
  • Mobile Edge Computing: Designing efficient resource-sharing schemes.
  • Large-Scale Distributed Optimization: Developing efficient and robust algorithms.
  • Security & Privacy in LLMs: Protecting large language models during training and application.
